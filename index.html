Q&A

What to wear?

Are children invited?

We love your families, but our wedding is for adults only


How do we get there?

Trains from Paddington and Marylebone or the Oxford Tube coach from Victoria, Marble Arch and Notting Hill


Any tips on where to stay?

There are lots of options for hotels, B&Bs, and Airbnbs. You can also find uni accommodation at University Rooms


Where should I park?

Parking in Oxford is limited, so check with your accommodation or there is a paid car park in St Clements
